# Protocol: Daily Lower Back Mobilization

## Quick Reference Card

**Daily (5 min) — do every morning or after prolonged sitting:**

1. 🐈 **Cat-Cow** — 10 reps, slow breath
2. 🦵 **Knee-to-Chest** — 30s hold, then 10 circles each direction
3. 🌀 **Supine Spinal Twist** — 20–30s each side
4. 🧘 **Child's Pose** — 60s, breathe into lower back

---

**3× per week (20 min):**

**Activate:**
- 90/90 Hip Switches × 10
- Dead Bug × 10 (5/side)
- Glute Bridge × 15 (2s hold)

**Mobilize:**
- World's Greatest Stretch × 5/side
- Hip Flexor Lunge Stretch × 60s/side
- Pigeon Pose × 60s/side
- Thoracic Extension over roller × 10 breaths

**Strengthen:**
- Bird Dog × 10/side
- Plank × 3 × 20–30s
- Clamshells × 15/side

**Cool-down:**
- Child's pose + spinal twist + breathing

---

## Coaching Notes

- Always warm up before the deeper session (the daily 5 min counts)
- Never push into sharp pain — discomfort is ok, pain is not
- Consistency > intensity — 5 min every day beats 60 min once a week
- Track: "Can I bend forward more comfortably than last week?"

## Progression Milestones

- Week 2: Pain-free Cat-Cow ✓
- Week 4: Plank 30s ✓
- Week 8: Add lightweight hip hinge (RDL with water bottles) ✓
- Week 12: 60s plank + full Bird Dog with control ✓
