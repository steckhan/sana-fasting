# Protocol: 5-Day Oat-Based Fasting

## Quick Reference Card

**Daily food:**
- Morning: 40–60g plain rolled oats in water + herbal tea
- Midday: 40g oats in water OR clear veggie broth (optional)
- Evening: 40g oats in water + herbal tea
- All day: 2–3L water / herbal tea

**Supplements allowed:**
- Magnesium 200–400mg at night
- Vitamin D (continue normal dose)
- Pinch sea salt in water if dizzy

---

## Day-by-Day Coaching Checklist

### ✅ Day 1 — Start
- [ ] Last solid meal the evening before
- [ ] Morning: oats + herbal tea
- [ ] 30-min gentle walk if energy ok
- [ ] Note: hunger normal, mild headache normal
- **Coach check-in:** How do you feel? Rate energy 1–10.

### ✅ Day 2 — Peak Hunger
- [ ] Continue protocol
- [ ] Epsom salt bath 20 min (optional but helpful)
- [ ] Rest more than usual
- **Coach check-in:** Hunger level? Any dizziness or unusual symptoms?

### ✅ Day 3 — Turning Point
- [ ] Mental clarity may arrive today
- [ ] Gentle walk or 5-min lower back routine if feeling ok
- [ ] Warm water with lemon in morning if sluggish digestion
- **Coach check-in:** Energy shift? Mental clarity? Emotional state?

### ✅ Day 4 — Metabolic Shift
- [ ] Ketone body production likely active
- [ ] Sleep quality often improves
- [ ] Continue gentle movement
- **Coach check-in:** How's sleep? Any strong cravings or mood changes?

### ✅ Day 5 — Final Day
- [ ] Prepare tomorrow's refeeding food NOW
- [ ] Gentle walk
- [ ] Journal: what did you notice? What do you want to change after?
- **Coach check-in:** Proud of yourself? What's your first refeeding meal?

---

## Refeeding (Days 6–8)

| Day | Food |
|-----|------|
| Day 6 morning | Small piece of fruit (apple, pear) |
| Day 6 midday | Steamed vegetables, broth |
| Day 6 evening | Vegetable soup |
| Day 7 | Add fermented food (kefir), small whole grain portion |
| Day 8 | Reintroduce eggs, fish, legumes |
| Day 9–10 | Gradual return to normal — no alcohol, no processed food |

---

## STOP Signs — Break the Fast Immediately

- Fainting or severe dizziness
- Heart palpitations or chest pain
- Extreme confusion
- Uncontrolled shaking
- Any symptom that frightens you

---

## Coaching Principles During Fast

1. **Daily check-in is mandatory** — flag any red flags immediately
2. **Celebrate every day** — this is genuinely hard
3. **Don't moralize food** — fasting is a tool, not a virtue
4. **Prepare for emotional content** — fasting often surfaces feelings
5. **The fast ends when it ends** — if someone needs to stop on day 3, that's ok
